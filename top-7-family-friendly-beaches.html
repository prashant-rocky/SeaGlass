Top 7 Family-Friendly Beaches in Kanagawa & the Shonan Coast

The Shonan Coast in Kanagawa Prefecture offers a range of beaches that are ideal for families seeking a relaxed seaside day near Tokyo. From the popular island of Enoshima to the quieter stretches of Zushi, these beaches combine safe swimming areas, convenient facilities, and nearby dining options. This guide highlights seven family-friendly beaches along the Shonan Coast, providing practical tips for a smooth and enjoyable visit.

1. Enoshima Beach

Enoshima Beach is one of the most popular destinations along the Shonan Coast. With a wide sandy area, gentle waves, and lifeguards on duty during the summer months, it is well-suited for families with children. The beach is also near the Enoshima Island attractions, including aquariums, gardens, and scenic viewpoints. Nearby cafés and restaurants offer casual dining with ocean views, making it easy to plan a full day of fun and relaxation.

2. Kamakura Yuigahama Beach

Yuigahama Beach, located in Kamakura, is known for its calm atmosphere and clean sand. Families can enjoy swimming, sandcastle building, and leisurely walks along the promenade. The beach is within walking distance of historical sites such as the Great Buddha and temples, allowing visitors to combine a beach day with cultural experiences. Facilities include restrooms, showers, and rental shops for umbrellas and beach chairs.

3. Zushi Beach

Zushi Beach offers a quieter alternative for families who prefer less crowded areas. The water is relatively shallow near the shore, making it suitable for children. Bicycle rentals and walking paths along the coastline provide additional activities for families. Local cafés and small eateries are available nearby, offering simple meals and refreshments throughout the day.

4. Shichirigahama Beach

Shichirigahama Beach is famous for its scenic views of Enoshima Island and Mount Fuji on clear days. The beach features soft sand and gentle waves, ideal for swimming and playing in the sand. While slightly smaller than other Shonan beaches, it offers a peaceful environment for families looking to escape larger crowds. Public restrooms and seasonal rental services are available to enhance convenience.

5. Katase Nishihama Beach

Katase Nishihama Beach is located near the Enoshima train station, making it highly accessible for day trips from Tokyo. This beach is well-equipped with lifeguards, shower facilities, and designated swimming zones. Families can enjoy beach sports, paddleboarding, and casual seaside walks. Nearby shops and restaurants make it easy to grab snacks or enjoy a relaxed meal without leaving the area.

6. Isshiki Beach

Isshiki Beach, slightly further from central Shonan, offers a peaceful coastal experience with shallow waters suitable for young children. Its natural surroundings provide space for picnics, casual games, and exploration. The beach has basic facilities, including restrooms and shaded areas, and visitors can find small local restaurants along the shore for meals and refreshments.

7. Hayama Beach

Hayama Beach is known for its scenic views and family-friendly environment. The calm waters and clean sand make it a safe choice for swimming and beach play. Picnic areas and nearby walking paths allow families to spend an entire day by the sea. Seasonal cafés and small shops in the vicinity provide snacks and light meals, enhancing the overall convenience for visitors.

Tips for Visiting Shonan Coast Beaches

When planning a family beach trip along the Shonan Coast, consider visiting early in the day to secure parking and quieter spots. Bring essentials such as sunscreen, hats, towels, and water. Many beaches offer seasonal rentals for umbrellas, beach chairs, and water sports gear. Check local ferry or train schedules if you plan to visit multiple beaches in one day to optimize your travel time.

Conclusion

The Shonan Coast in Kanagawa Prefecture provides a variety of family-friendly beaches with accessible facilities, calm waters, and nearby amenities. From the bustling Enoshima to the serene Hayama, these beaches offer options for both relaxation and activities. Planning ahead and choosing beaches suited to your family’s needs ensures a memorable and enjoyable seaside experience near Tokyo.
